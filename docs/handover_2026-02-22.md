## 4. Critical Context
- **Layout Math:** If you ever touch the `FloatingTabBar` or Audio Pills, DO NOT use `paddingBottom: insets.bottom` on wrappers. We strictly use `bottom: Math.max(insets.bottom, 16) + offset` combined with rigid component heights (`height: 64`) to calculate vertical stacks. This preserves Apple Music-style iOS floats.
- **Shadows on Blur:** NEVER apply a standard iOS drop shadow directly to an `expo-blur` `BlurView`. iOS cannot render hollow transparent shadows, so it creates a massive opaque black rectangle underneath the view that ruins the glass effect. Always use `overflow: 'hidden'` on the blur body itself.
- **Color Identity:** The user heavily prefers a premium, profound dark mode (`#09090B` Zinc). Do NOT revert to "blue-tinted" dark modes. The violet and gold brand colors must strictly serve as *accents*, not backgrounds.
